Boursin and Ham Pasta

Description

This creamy Boursin pasta with crispy bacon is quick and easy, perfect for busy weeknights!

Ingredients
  • Boursin
  • Heavy sauce
  • Pasta
  • Ham
  • Parmesan
  • Parsley
  • Garlic
Steps
  1. Cook the pasta as you like them
  2. In a frying pan, fry the chopped ham and chopped garlic in some olive oil for a few minutes until ham is cooked and crisp
  3. Add the heavy cream and the boursin cheese
  4. Add Salt and Pepper
  5. Incorporate the cooked pasta into the sauce
  6. Add the chopped parsley and some parmesan in it
  7. Let it cook for 5 minutes at a low heating fire
  8. You can serve and eat
