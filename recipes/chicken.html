Chicken Curry

Description

The Chicken Curry is a south-asian meal from India. This easy staple chicken curry is a fantastic recipe for family dinners. It's made with just a handful of ingredients and is enriched with creamy yogurt.

Ingredients
  • Rice
  • Chicken
  • Curry
  • Heavy Cream
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cumin
Steps
  1. Cut Chicken in cube
  2. Rince rice and put it into boiling water
  3. Cook chicken in a pan to make it brown with some oil, add Heavy Cream and Curry
  4. Add Salt, Pepper and Cumin to the chicken
  5. Let it cook for 5 minutes at a low heating fire
  6. Drain the rice
  7. Add everything inside the pan to the rice, let it cook at low heating fire for 20 minutes while blending it every 2 minutes
  8. You can serve and eat
